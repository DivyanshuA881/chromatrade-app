## ✨ About The Game

ChromaTrade is an interactive **color stock trading game** built as a Telegram Mini App where players can buy and sell colored assets in a live fluctuating market. Watch prices change every 3 seconds and build your trading portfolio!

### 🎮 Features

- ✅ **Live Market Updates** - Prices change randomly every 3 seconds (-5% to +8%)
- ✅ **6 Unique Colors** - Crimson Red, Deep Blue, Meadow Green, Solar Yellow, Royal Purple, Tangerine
- ✅ **User Profile System** - Custom user ID with unique identifier
- ✅ **Add/Withdraw Funds** - Manage your in-game balance
- ✅ **Real-time Portfolio Tracking** - See your total value update instantly
- ✅ **Price Change Indicators** - Green (↑) for gains, Red (↓) for losses
- ✅ **Telegram Integration** - Works seamlessly as a Telegram Mini App
- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Works on all devices (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop)
- ✅ **Local Storage Save** - Best score is saved in your browser
- ✅ **Dark Theme** - Beautiful dark/neon interface

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Start with $250** initial cash balance
2. **Buy colors low** - Click "BUY" when prices drop
3. **Sell colors high** - Click "SELL" when prices rise
4. **Check your profile** - Click "MY WALLET" to view holdings and manage balance
5. **Add funds** - Use the profile drawer to add more trading cash
6. **Build wealth** - Grow your portfolio value!

### 📊 Game Mechanics

- **Price Updates:** Automatic every 3 seconds
- **Price Range:** $0.50 - $500 per share
- **Initial Cash:** $250
- **Win Strategy:** Buy during price dips, sell during peaks
